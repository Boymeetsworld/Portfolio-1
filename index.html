Introducing...

Ayden Scott is a poet and Nonfiction Writer based out of Atlanta with over twelve years of experience working in spoken word and written poetry.

He primarily works on confessional and slice of life pieces which frequently focusing on mental health, black empowerment, and coming of age moments.

He is formally trained by the Chicago High School Of The Arts. He's been published by multiple sites and organizations such as The 2017 After School Matters Anthology and Vocal Media.

Currently, he is in the process of writing his first full length anthology

"The View From The Glass Tower"
